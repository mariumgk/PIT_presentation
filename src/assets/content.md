## 03 Introduction
Companies are no longer judged only by salaries. Work environment, employee wellbeing, learning opportunities, and work-life balance play a major role in attracting and retaining talented employees.

This presentation focuses on S&P Global, a leading global company known for data, analytics, and financial intelligence services.

Purpose: analyze how S&P Global supports employees beyond salary through perks, benefits, work culture, professional development, and X-Factors.

## 14 The X-Factors
Unique differentiators:
1. EssentialTECH Program
2. Global Career Coaching for all employees
3. Student Loan Matching Contributions
4. Hybrid Work + Work Your Way Culture
5. Recharge unlimited flexible time off

Why they matter:
S&P Global invests in transforming its own workforce, provides coaching beyond senior leadership, supports education debt, integrates flexibility into work culture, and trusts employees to manage time responsibly.
